Our mission is to aid the discovery, creation and dissemination of new cultural forms and spaces by nourishing interdisciplinary collaborations. We organise multifaceted educational programs, open exhibitions and talks. Art Related works with cultural associations in Europe and beyond and supports the program of public and private institutions. The foundation is developing creative and educational projects that diversify the cultural landscape in Bulgaria by promoting transnational artistic exchange.

In 2019, we extended our network through partnerships with Wikimedia Deutschland, Creative Europe Desk – Bulgaria, Resonator – Hub for Creative Innovations and Sofia Art Week.
